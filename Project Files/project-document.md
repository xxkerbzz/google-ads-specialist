## 1. Executive Summary

### Business Overview
LeadGen Ads is a specialized Google Ads management service that helps local service businesses get more qualified leads. As a solo consultancy, we offer hands-on, personalized campaign management—no account managers, no offshore teams, just direct access to someone who actually knows your business.

### Target Market
Primary focus on home service businesses including:
- HVAC companies
- Plumbing services
- Electrical contractors
- Roofing companies
- Landscaping businesses
- Home remodeling contractors
- Pest control services
- Cleaning services

### Unique Value Proposition
- **Specialization**: Exclusive focus on local service businesses (no e-commerce, SaaS, or B2B tech)
- **Personal Service**: Direct access to the specialist, no account managers or offshore teams
- **Performance-Based**: No long-term contracts, month-to-month engagement
- **Local Expertise**: Deep understanding of local service business dynamics and customer acquisition
- **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on qualified leads that convert, not just traffic volume

## 2. Business Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Lead Generation**: Generate 20-30 qualified consultation requests per month
2. **Client Acquisition**: Convert 15-20% of consultations into paying clients
3. **Client Retention**: Maintain 85%+ monthly retention rate
4. **Revenue**: Build recurring revenue base of $15,000-25,000/month within 12 months
5. **Authority**: Establish as go-to specialist for service business Google Ads

- Consultation show rate: 70%+

- Client lifetime value: $12,000+ (avg 8-month retention)
- Client satisfaction: 4.5+ star average rating

**Buying Behavior:**
- Research-oriented (reads reviews, case studies)

## 5. Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

#### Large Digital Marketing Agencies
**Strengths:**
- Established brand recognition
- Full-service offerings
- Large teams and resources
- Case studies and testimonials

**Weaknesses:**
- High overhead = higher prices
- Junior account managers handle accounts
- One-size-fits-all approach
- Long-term contracts required
- Slow to respond and optimize

#### Other Freelance PPC Specialists
**Strengths:**
- Personal service
- Competitive pricing
- Flexible contracts

**Weaknesses:**
- Generalists (work with all industries)
- Limited capacity
- Variable expertise levels
- Less structured processes

#### DIY Solutions (Business Owner Self-Management)
**Strengths:**
- No management fees
- Full control
- Direct knowledge of business

**Weaknesses:**
- Time-intensive
- Steep learning curve
- Costly mistakes
- Suboptimal performance
- Difficulty staying current with platform changes

1. **Niche Specialization**: We only work with service businesses, so we understand what actually works
2. **Proven Results**: Real track record with specific, measurable outcomes—not just promises
3. **No Contracts**: Month-to-month means you can leave anytime if it's not working
4. **Direct Access**: You work directly with the specialist, not a junior account manager
5. **Local Expertise**: We get how local service businesses work and what their customers need
6. **Quality Over Quantity**: We focus on leads that actually convert, not just traffic numbers

## 9. Technical Stack (Website)

### Recommended Technology
- **Framework**: Next.js 16+ (App Router, React-based, SEO-friendly)
- **Language**: TypeScript (type safety, better DX)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (rapid development, consistent design)
- **Hosting**: Vercel (optimized for Next.js, easy deployment)
- **Content**: Markdown files in `/content` directory (v6 agents workflow)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console
- **Forms**: Formspree or SendGrid API
- **Scheduling**: Calendly integration

**Note**: Built using v6 Agents pipeline (PRD → Structure → Builder) with template-based SEO content architecture.
